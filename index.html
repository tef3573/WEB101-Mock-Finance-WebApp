About Us


Tanesha's Market Madness is a digital space where finance meets technology for people who enjoy exploring numbers and movements. It's a hub for exploring markets, data, and innovation in real time. We blend real-time market energy with clear analysis-part simulation, part data experiment, part think-piece collection. So, whether you're a student, analyst, or a curious observer, our goal is spark curiosity and clarity about how money moves and why it matter through the evolving relationship between finance, tech, and human behavior.

The Aura Capital Fund Live Discussion

Join us for an exclusive sit-down with Aura Capital Fund’s CEO Jeanmark Ceant, as we unpack their mission, strategy, and the values driving their next big move — going public on the stock exchange. Aura Capital Fund is a pioneering mutual fund built on ESG principles, focusing on companies that are not only profitable, but ethical, community-driven, and sustainable. Their portfolio centers on Black-owned and Black-led businesses, investing in innovation, equity, and long-term value creation.
