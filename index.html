The Aura Capital Fund Live Discussion

Join us for an exclusive sit-down with Aura Capital Fund’s CEO Jeanmark Ceant, as we unpack their mission, strategy, and the values driving their next big move — going public on the stock exchange. Aura Capital Fund is a pioneering mutual fund built on ESG principles, focusing on companies that are not only profitable, but ethical, community-driven, and sustainable. Their portfolio centers on Black-owned and Black-led businesses, investing in innovation, equity, and long-term value creation.
